I confess that as a Montessori parent, I prefer toys that encourage her own imagination. The blinking, beeping and talking toys can be fun, but I guess I’m old school. GO

We like to go places. We like to take Lotte with us. Before she turned 2, she had already been to New York, Chicago, Hong Kong, Thailand, Vancouver, Paris and London. She has a Nexus card. If we can instill anything into Lotte at all, it would be the love of food and travel. And when we take her places both at home and abroad, there are 2 things we would never think of leaving home without...

GEAR

It's a fact of parenting life: Have kid, will be burdened with stuff. It's impossible to go anywhere reasonably prepared without lugging half your possessions with you. But if there are items that are a joy to use, or items that just make your life easier, then you are more likely to remember bringing them, and more likely to enjoy your time out with your kid.
